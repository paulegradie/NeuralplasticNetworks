## Explicit Non-Claims

- The current evidence does not show solved continual learning.
- The current evidence does not show context gating is novel by itself.
- The current evidence does not show a complete hippocampal or biological theory.
- The current evidence does not show raw sensory latent-world discovery or end-to-end perceptual learning.
- The current evidence does not show inference of unseen primitive transitions.
- Exp13.2 does not provide neural baselines.
- Exp14 does not outperform an oracle context-gated upper bound.
